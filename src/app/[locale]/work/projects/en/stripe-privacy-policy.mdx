**Stripe Privacy Policy**

At Techie Neighbor LLC, we're committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your online choices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

**What Do We Collect?**

When you visit our website, we may collect information about you through the following methods:

1. **Registration and Sign-up**: If you create an account or sign up for a service on our website, we'll collect your name, email address, password, and other contact information.
2. **Payment Information**: When you make a payment through us, we'll collect your credit card number, expiration date, CVV code (if applicable), and other payment details.
3. **User Feedback**: We may collect any feedback or comments from you about our website or services.

**How Do You Use Stripe?**

Once we've collected your information, you can use it in the following ways:

1. **Make Payments**: Use your credit card to make payments through our platform.
2. **Create and Manage Accounts**: Create an account on our website and manage your payment settings.
3. **View and Download Content**: Access content and resources on our website.

**How Do We Protect Your Data?**

We take the security of your personal data very seriously. To protect it, we:

1. **Use Industry-Standard Security Measures**: We use encryption, secure servers, and other measures to protect your information.
2. **Limit Access**: Only authorized personnel have access to your payment information and user accounts.
3. **Regularly Update and Patch Our Systems**: We continuously work to improve the security of our systems to prevent unauthorized access.

**What Happens If I Leave Stripe?**

If you choose to leave our website, your account will be closed, and you won't be able to make payments through us again. However:

1. **Your Payment Information Will Remain Secure**: Even after you leave our platform, your payment information remains secure with Stripe.
2. **Stripe May Update Our Terms and Conditions**: At any time, we may modify this Privacy Policy or update it to reflect changes in our services.

**How Do You Opt-Out?**

You can opt-out of certain types of data collection by:

1. **Logging Out**: If you're not a Stripe customer, you can log out of your account on our website.
2. **Using a Third-Party Cookie Manager**: Some browsers allow you to manage cookies; check your browser settings.

**Can I Access My Data?**

You have the right to access and view your personal data that we hold on you. To do this:

1. **Contact Us**: Reach out to us at support@techieneighbor.net or 470-665-4100.
2. **Use a Third-Party Data Porting Tool**: Some tools allow you to transfer your Stripe account information.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we'll notify you of any changes via email or notification on our website.
